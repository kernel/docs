title: "Enterprise SLAs"
---

Kernel offers tiered enterprise support with defined response times, dedicated channels, and account management. All enterprise tiers include everything in the base [Enterprise plan](/info/pricing).

## Support tiers

### Scale 200

- Slack Connect channel
- 4-hour response time during business hours
- Monthly usage reviews

### Scale 500

Everything in Scale 200, plus:

- 1-hour response time during business hours
- Dedicated account manager
- Quarterly business reviews

### Scale 1000

Everything in Scale 500, plus:

- 30-minute response time, 24/7
- Custom SLA agreements
- Priority incident escalation

### Custom

Everything in Scale 1000, plus:

- 15-minute response time, 24/7
- Dedicated infrastructure options
- Custom compliance and security reviews

## Priority levels

| Priority | Description | Scale 200 | Scale 500 | Scale 1000 | Custom |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| P1 — Outage | Service is down or critically impaired | 4 hours (business hours) | 1 hour (business hours) | 30 minutes (24/7) | 15 minutes (24/7) |
| P2 — Bug | Functional issue impacting workflows | 4 hours (business hours) | 1 hour (business hours) | 30 minutes (24/7) | 15 minutes (24/7) |
| P3 — General | Questions, guidance, feature requests | 1 business day | 1 business day | 4 hours (business hours) | 1 hour (business hours) |

## Support channels

All enterprise tiers include a dedicated Slack Connect channel for direct communication with the Kernel team. Higher tiers add a dedicated account manager and structured review cadences.

## Additional offerings

- **Usage reviews** — monthly or quarterly reviews of browser usage, concurrency, and cost optimization opportunities
- **Custom SLA agreements** — tailored response times and uptime commitments for Scale 1000 and above
- **Compliance and security reviews** — custom security assessments and compliance documentation for regulated industries
- **Dedicated infrastructure** — isolated infrastructure options available on Custom tier
